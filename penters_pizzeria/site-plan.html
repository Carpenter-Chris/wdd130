Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a website for fictitious pizza restaurant. This sites main purpose will be to inform views about the pizza shop. Show images, give general information, menu items, location, contact info, about page, etc.

Audience

The audience will be the general public who are looking for a delicious pizza! They may be wanting to know more about menu items, where to find the pizzeria, how to order, prices, take out or delivery options.

About Us

About page

Welcome to Penter’s Pizzeria!

We are a family owned and operated pizzeria. We love pizza and want to share out love of pizza with you and our community! We have been in business at the same location for the last 15 years. From day 1 we have strived to make the best food possible. We have always put the best possible ingredients and refined our methods over time.

The pizzeria love to support the local community. We participate in many of the community events. We donate to the local after school community centers. We work with local sports groups and teams to help raise money. We will also participate in community events and festivals. If you have any suggestions on other opportunities to support the community, please let us know!

We are grateful for the wonderful community and friends we have made over the years. We look forward to continuing to serve the community and share our love for pizza for another 15 years! Thank you all for your amazing support!
